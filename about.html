Welcome to Good Shepherd College

Good Shepherd College is a Christian-centered academic institution located in Mhondoro-Ngezi, Zimbabwe. Our mission is to foster holistic growth, discipline, and leadership through quality education.

  • We offer full Ordinary and Advanced Level curricula.
  • Vacant places are currently available for day scholars.
  • Boarding school vacancies will be open from next academic year.
  • Our motto is Ebenezer – "Thus far the Lord has helped us."
