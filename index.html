About Good Shepherd College

At Good Shepherd College, we are committed to academic excellence grounded in Christian values. Our mission is to nurture holistic growth and leadership among Zimbabwean learners.

  • We offer a full curriculum for both Ordinary Level (Form 1–4) and Advanced Level (Lower Six & Upper Six).
  • Qualified, dedicated teachers who guide learners in both academics and character development.
  • Supportive and disciplined learning environment built on Christian faith and the motto: Ebenezer — "Thus far the Lord has helped us".

Why Choose Good Shepherd College?

At Good Shepherd College, we offer a nurturing and disciplined environment where students thrive academically, spiritually, and socially. With a strong foundation in Christian values and a focus on holistic education, we are committed to developing future leaders of Zimbabwe and beyond.

Academic Excellence

We deliver quality education at both O' Level and A' Level with consistently high pass rates and a qualified teaching staff.

Christian Values

Our students are guided by strong moral principles, grounded in faith and our motto: Ebenezer.

Supportive Environment

We create a safe and supportive space that encourages self-discipline, confidence, and leadership potential in every learner.

Courses

HEXCO Vocational Programs

Garment Construction

Textile and Design Technology

This course equips learners with skills in sewing, pattern drafting, fashion illustration, and garment finishing. It promotes creativity, precision, and practical competence in clothing and textile production.

Bakery

Bakery Studies

Learners gain hands-on experience in baking techniques including bread, cakes, and pastries. Emphasis is placed on food safety, ingredient handling, and entrepreneurial skills relevant to Zimbabwe’s hospitality industry.

Computers

Computer Operations and Packages

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of computing, including Microsoft Office packages, file management, typing skills, and basic ICT literacy needed for today’s digital workplace.
